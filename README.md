<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Artificial%20Intelligence-E5F2E5?style=flat-square&labelColor=F8F4E8&color=93B49A" alt="Artificial Intelligence"/>

<img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Agricultural%20Technology-F2F0DC?style=flat-square&labelColor=F8F4E8&color=B8B67D" alt="Agricultural Technology"/>

</div>

<br/>

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 📚 Research Focus

This research reviews and comparatively evaluates image-based methods used to detect coconut ripeness and determine quality grades.

The study explores how **computer vision**, **image processing**, and **deep learning techniques** can improve the accuracy, consistency, and efficiency of traditional coconut grading processes.

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### 🚀 Final-Year Research Direction

**AI-Based Coconut Type Recognition, Ripeness Index Prediction and Export Grade Recommendation**

The proposed solution focuses on developing an intelligent system that can:

* Recognize coconut types from images
* Predict a coconut ripeness index
* Recommend suitable export grades
* Support faster and more consistent quality evaluation
* Contribute to smart agricultural decision-making

</td>
</tr>
</table>

<br/>

<div align="center">

> 🌿 **Research vision:** Applying artificial intelligence to strengthen agricultural technology, improve quality assessment, and create practical solutions for industry challenges.
